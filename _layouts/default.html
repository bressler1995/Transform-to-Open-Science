NASA's Transform to Open Science Initiative (TOPS) is excited to host a community of scientists from different disciplines and backgrounds. By fostering an inclusive culture of Open Science, we hope to accelerate major discoveries, broaden participation from historically excluded groups, and increase the adoption of open science principles. We encourage you to take our OS101 curriculum to increase the impact of your research and we hope to hear from you in our GitHub discussions or through success stories you may have, which we will publish on our monthly newsletter.
